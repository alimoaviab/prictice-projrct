## PRODUCT SUMMARY

EduPlexo is a comprehensive AI-powered school management system (School ERP) designed for educational institutions in Pakistan and worldwide. It provides a unified digital platform to manage all school operations including student enrollment, attendance tracking, fee collection, exam management, result generation, homework assignment, timetable scheduling, parent communication, and more. The platform serves schools, colleges, academies, madrasas, and any educational institution seeking to digitize their operations.
