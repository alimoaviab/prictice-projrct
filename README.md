## Feature Coverage

- Academic years, classes, subjects, and enrollments
- Attendance marking, viewing, and reports
- Fee configuration, monthly generation, and payments
- Exams, marks entry, grading, and result publication
- Parent portal summaries for children, homework, and fees

## Endpoint Prefixes

- `/api/school/*` - admin and school operations
- `/api/teacher/*` - teacher operations
- `/api/parent/*` - parent and student portal views
