## 💡 QUICK TIPS

### For Best Results:

✅ **Be Specific**
```
Good: "Show me Grade 6 attendance"
Bad:  "Show me something"
```

✅ **Use Names**
```
Good: "Show me Ali Ahmed's fee status"
Bad:  "Show me that student's fees"
```

✅ **Ask One Thing at a Time**
```
Good: "Show me today's attendance"
Then: "Which students are absent?"
Bad:  "Show me attendance and fees and exams"
```

✅ **Use Any Language**
```
✅ English: "Show me classes"
✅ Urdu: "کلاسز دکھائیں"
✅ Mixed: "Classes dikhao"
```

## 🌟 EXAMPLE CONVERSATIONS

### Conversation 1: Morning Check

```
You: "What is happening today?"
Bot: Shows today's schedule, attendance, live classes

You: "Show me Grade 6 attendance"
Bot: Shows Grade 6 attendance with present/absent count

You: "Who is absent?"
Bot: Lists absent students with names
```

### Conversation 2: Student Inquiry

```
You: "Show me Ali Ahmed's details"

You: "What is his attendance?"
```
