## 💡 TIPS FOR ASKING QUESTIONS

### ✅ Good Questions (اچھے سوالات)

```
✅ Specific: "Show me Grade 6 attendance"
✅ Clear: "How do I create a class?"
✅ Direct: "What is Ali Ahmed's fee status?"
✅ Contextual: "Show me today's timetable for Grade 6"
```

### ❌ Avoid These (یہ نہ پوچھیں)

```
❌ Too vague: "Tell me something"
❌ Unrelated: "What's the weather?"
❌ Off-topic: "Tell me a joke"
❌ Incomplete: "Show me..."
```

---

## 🎯 RESPONSE TYPES

### 1. Data Responses
Chatbot will call tools and show you real data:
```
User: "How many classes are there?"
Bot: "Your school currently has 12 classes:
     • Grade 1 (A, B) — 45 students
     • Grade 2 (A, B) — 52 students
     ..."
```

### 2. How-To Responses
Chatbot will give step-by-step instructions:
```
User: "How do I create a class?"
Bot: "Creating a class is easy! Follow these steps:
     1. Go to Dashboard → Classes
     2. Click 'Add New Class'
```
